Frequently Asked Questions

What is Arali?

Arali is a retail management platform that helps small shop owners manage inventory, track sales, reduce waste, and make better daily decisions. Learn more about Arali’s core features.

Who is Arali built for?

Arali is designed for small and medium retail stores, including kirana shops, grocery stores, and local retailers.

Does Arali work on mobile?

Yes. Arali works on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

How does Arali help reduce inventory waste?

Arali monitors stock levels, expiry dates, and sales trends to help shop owners take action before products expire or run out of stock.

Is Arali free to use?

Arali offers a free plan to get started, with optional paid plans for advanced features.
